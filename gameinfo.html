Game Overview:

The year is 2113 and we are in a period of intergalactic civil war.
TA band of Rebels, who call themselves The Free, have just struck their biggest blow yet to the Chosen by assassinating Superior Leader Glough at his yearly address.
Elsewhere, they have also managed to infiltrate the prison system and have orchestrated a mass breakout of The Free from the Goldengate Detention Centre.
There, we join Isias, caught up in the very same cell block The Free are rampaging through. He wishes to escape his inevitable demise at the hands of the Chosen for failing to protect his charge - Superior Leader Glough himself.
Follow Isias on his path to redemption through the world of The Chosen in :PROJECT: Game
